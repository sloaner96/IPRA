extra News

The success of this month in...Parks & Recreation has led to the creation of extra. As needed, extra eNewsletters will be sent to update the IPRA membership of meetings, events and news of interest.

The deadline for the monthly eNewsletter is still one month earlier than the date of distribution. However, if you miss getting into the main eNewsletter you can still submit a request by completing the form found here.

IPRA hires Professional Services Director
Mike Selep has joined the IPRA team this month, from the Fox Valley Special Recreation Association, as the new Professional Services Director! Mike will oversee member recruitment and retention, act as a liaison between IPRA and its committees and sections, run the annual Western Open volunteer force, and more.

Please help us welcome Mike Selep. He can be reached by email at mike@ilipra.org.

Increase revenue, participation and general traffic
The Joint Public Awareness Committee created a website a couple of years ago which allows Park Districts to input their facilities and events...for FREE. By posting your agency's events and facility offerings on this website the general public can now take advantage of all your events.

PLEASE go to illinoisparksandrecreation.com and update the calendar listing with your events.  JPAC has been doing community outreach by distributing magnets & PR pieces to promote the site, but we need you to help post your events.

This is an excellent way to increase revenue, participation and general traffic within your programs, parks & services--and it's FREE. Don't miss out on this great opportunity.

Are you looking for a mentor?
If so, there are plenty of professionals willing to help. Check out the mentoring page on the IPRA website to find out which mentors are available from you to choose from. The process is very easy, go onto the site and click “Available Mentors” and you can look at the bio’s from the mentors to pick from.

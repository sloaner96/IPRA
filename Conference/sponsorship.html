2004 Conference Sponsorship Programs

 

Steer prospects your way!

Reach more attendees — more effectively!

It is the joint responsibility of IAPD/IPRA to attract attendees to the Conference.

It is, however, the sole responsibility of exhibitors to attract attendees to their booth.

Our sponsorship programs are designed to help you do so.

The 2004 Conference is expected to attract Elected Officials and Professionals in park and forest preserve districts, municipal park and recreation departments, conservation, special recreation associations and other private recreation agencies.

Our sponsorships will help you gain a competitive edge and are available to accommodate any size budget.
